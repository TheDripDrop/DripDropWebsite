About Us

Overview: The DripDrop is an edible ring made out of waffle cone material that protects ice cream drips from getting on your hands and clothes. The inventors, founders, and CEOs of The DripDrop are Sam Nassif and Oliver Greenwald. The DripDrop company is based in Denver, Colorado.
Our Story: One day we were walking past an ice cream store and we saw these little kids with drippy hands and ice cream all over their clothes. Then we noticed how their mom used a handful of napkins to attempt to clean them up. We kept walking past the store horrified at the image and after a few steps we both turned to each other and knew we had to do something about this problem. And thatâ€™s how The DripDrop started.
Sweet Success: Sam and Oliver entered an intensive invention competition by The Charles Gates Foundation with 100 contestants at the age of 10 and won 2nd place with their invention The DripDrop. They spent the next 3 years trying to receive a patent and are now proud patent holders of a U.S. Patent!
